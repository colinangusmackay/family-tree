## Text

> 1866, MARRIAGES in the District of Dornoch in the County of Sutherland
>
> <br/>
>
> No. 1
>
> When, Where and How Married: 1866 on the Nineth day of February at Dornoch; After Banns according to the forms of the Free Church
>
> <br/>
>
> GROOM
>
> Signed: Peter Fraser
>
> Profession: Fisherman
>
> Condition: Bachelor
>
> Age: 19
>
> Usual Residence: Embo, Parish of Dornoch
>
> Name, Surname and Profession of Father: Keneth Fraser, Fisherman
>
> Name and Maiden Surname of Mother: Bell Fraser M. S. Mackay
>
> <br/>
>
> BRIDE
>
> Signed: Christina Mackay, Her X Mark; David Ross, Witness; George Matheson, Witness
>
> Condition: (Spinster)
>
> Age: 18
>
> Usual Residence: Embo, Parish of Dornoch
>
> Name, Surname, and Profession of Father: Donald Mackay, Fisherman
>
> Name and Maiden Surname of Mother: Ann Mackay M.S. Mackay
>
> <br/>
>
> Signature of Officiating Minister: George Kennedy, Minister of the Free Church of Dornoch
>
> Witness: Donald Ross
>
> Witness: George Matheson
>
> <br/>
>
> When and Where Registered: 1866 February 9th at Dornoch
>
> Regisrar: Charles Neill, Registrar
