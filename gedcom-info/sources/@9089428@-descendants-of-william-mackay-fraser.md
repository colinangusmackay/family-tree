> Descendants of William Mackay Fraser
>
> <br/>
>
> In tracing this Fraser family from Embo,Sutherlandshire, Scotland, we have encountered several hurdles; one of which is the fact that their names were most likely gaelic originally. The records found at Scotland's People are in english and translations of names, and nick names seem to vary. As well, Embo is also referred to as Fishertown, Dornoch in some documents.
>
> Please advise us of any corrections to the following:
>
