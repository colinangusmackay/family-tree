> 1949 DEATHS in the DISTRICT of PAISLEY in the COUNTY of RENFREW
>
> <br/>
>
> No. 697
>
> Name and Surname: Janet Hanton
>
> Condition: Widow of James Hanton, Packing case maker (journeyman)
>

>

>
