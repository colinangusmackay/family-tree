## Text

> No. 321058
>
> 1926
>
> Marriage License
>
> Wayne County, Michigan
>
> 82 14796
>
> ---
>
> To any person legally authorized to solemnize marriage
>
> Greeting:
>
> **Marraige May Be Solemnized Between**
>
> Mr. Kenneth McKay and M Helen Morrison, affidavit having been filed in this office, as provided by Public Act ____ 128 Laws of 1887, as amended, by which it appears that said Kenneth McKay is 32 years of age, color is White, residence is Detroit, Michigan, and birthplace was Scotland, occupation is Mechanic, father's name James, and mother's maiden name was Isabella McKay, has been previously married No times; and that said Helen Morrison is 26 years of age, color is White, residence is Grosse Pointe, Michigan, and birthplace was Scotland occupation is Maid, father's name John, and mother's maiden name was Helen Copeland and who has previously married No times, and whoes maiden name was [not populated], and whose [parent or guardian not populated] consent, in case she has not attained the age of eighteen years, has been filed in my office.
>
> <br/>
>
> **In Witness Thereof**, I have herunto attached my hand and the seal of Wayne County, Michigan, this 22nd day of October, A. D. 1926
>
> Thos. F. Farrell, County Clerk, by J.G. Corkins, Dep. Clerk
>
> ---
>
> **Certificate of Marriage**
>
> Between Kenenth McKay and M Helen Morrison
>
> **I hereby certify** that ,in accordance with the above license, the persons herein mentioned were joined in marriage by me, at Detroit, County of Wayne, Michigan, on the 28th day of Oct., A.D. 1926, in the presence of Elizabeth Morrison or Grosse Pointe, Mich. and Roderick MacLeod, of Detroit, Mich.
>
> <br/>
>
> R. W. Mansfield, Minister.
>
