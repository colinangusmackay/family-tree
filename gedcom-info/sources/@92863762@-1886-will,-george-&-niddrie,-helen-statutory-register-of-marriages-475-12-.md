## Text

> 1886 MARRIAGES in the Parish of Campsie, in the County of Stirling
>
> <br/>
>
> No. 12
>
> When, Where and How Married: 1886 on the fourth day of June at Glenmiln, Campsie; After publication, according to the forms of the Church of Scotland.
>
> <br/>
>
> GROOM:
>
> Signed: George Will
>
> Profession: Railway Porter
>
> Condition: Bachelor
>
> Age: 25
>
> Usual Residence: 7 Craigton Terrace, Springburn
>
> Name, Surname and Profession of Father: David WIll, Journeyman Joiner (deceased)
>
> Name and Maiden Surname of Mother: Jane Will M.S. Milne
>
> <br/>
>
> BRIDE:
>
> Signed: Helen Niddrie
>
> Profession: Domestic Servant
>
> Condition (spinster)
>
> Age: 25
>
> Usual Residence: Glenmiln, Campsie
>
> Name, Surname and Profession of Father: David Niddrie
>
> Name and Maiden Surname of Mother: Jessie Niddrie M.S. Milne
>
> <br/>
>
> Officiating Minister: John Young Scott, Minister of Campsie
>
> Witness: Stewart Niddrie
>
> Witness: William Forrester
>
> When and Where Registered: 1886 June 5th at Campsie
>
> Registrar: M. Baird
>
